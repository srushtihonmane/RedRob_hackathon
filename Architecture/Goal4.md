### Goal 4 ‚Äî Candidate‚ÜîJD scoring ‚Äî ‚úÖ FULLY DETAILED & LOCKED (D1‚ÄìD9, incl. D6.5)
**SCOPE:** Goal 4 = the **relevance estimate**. It owns retrieval fusion, feature transforms, criterion satisfaction, corroboration, dense/lexical/CE fusion, and emits a **fit bundle**: `fit_score`, component subscores, a per-criterion **satisfaction degree + evidence trail** (feeds Goal 7), a bounded `engagement_modifier`, and a bounded `risk_modifier` (with sub-components exposed). It does **not** make decisions. **Conceptual hierarchy (locked):** Goal 4 = relevance, Goal 5 = risk (hard trap/honeypot/disqualifier *detection*), Goal 6 = decision policy. Goal 6 computes `final_score = fit_score √ó engagement_modifier √ó risk_modifier` subject to `hard_gate==false ‚àß honeypot==false`. **Soft risk shapes the score inside Goal 4; hard risk excludes inside Goal 6** ‚Äî different mechanisms, no overlap.

## Internal module decomposition (implementation structure ‚Äî not new goals)
Goal 4 is the largest unit and the relevance engine, so it is built as **five internal
modules in a pipeline**, each independently testable and ablatable (the D9 audit loop
relies on this). These are modules **within** Goal 4 ‚Äî no renumbering, no new goal IDs.
The D-decisions map onto them; build and test them in this order.

- **4A Retrieval (D3):** generous recall-first **union** shortlist (`dense_evidence ‚à™ dense_identity ‚à™ BM25 ‚à™ structural_prefilter`), membership-only, no exclusionary gates. Interface out: shortlist ids. Test: every planted Tier-5/strong-single-channel candidate is in the shortlist.
- **4B Criterion evaluation (D4 transforms + D6 importance + D7 corroboration):** for each typed criterion, gather evidence across {assessment, title, skillmeta} (static) + {dense, lexical} (runtime), apply trust-tiered noisy-OR + soft corroboration gate ‚Üí **satisfaction degree + evidence trail**. Owns feature transforms (quantile/rank-norm on frozen pool stats; band features excluded ‚Üí membership functions). Interface out: per-criterion `{satisfaction_degree, evidence[]}`. Test: stuffer (Tier-3-only) ‚Üí ~floor; plain-language Tier-5 (Tier-2) ‚Üí satisfied.
- **4C Fusion (D5 spine + D6):** combine criterion satisfactions into `structured_criteria_score = MH_softAND √ó (1 + NH_bonus) √ó CM_modifier` (criterion-satisfaction is the spine; BM25/dense/CE are evidence sources, not independent additive channels). Interface out: raw structured fit. Test: a high dense-cosine alone cannot inflate fit.
- **4D Calibration (D5):** tail-anchored calibration against full-pool distributions (pin irrelevant bulk‚Üí0, reward upper tail‚Üí1) ‚Üí `fit_score`; bucket `fit_tier ‚àà {Elite,Strong,Plausible,Filler}` on calibrated fit (stable across the 3 weight sets). Interface out: `fit_score`, `fit_tier`, `fit_contribution` per criterion. Test: irrelevant median maps near 0, not 0.5.
- **4E Modifiers (D6.5 + D8):** `engagement_modifier` ‚àà [0.7,1.1] (availability/responsiveness, missing‚Üíneutral) and `risk_modifier` ‚àà [0.5,1.0] (stuffer geometry + archetype resemblance, monotone, no overrides). Both **bounded, separable, applied in Goal 6** ‚Äî never fused into fit. Test: low-fit √ó high-engagement stays low (kills the HR-Manager trap).
- **Cross-module wrapper ‚Äî D9 eval/self-labeling harness:** audit scorecards + the trap/adversarial unit-test suite + the ~250‚Äì400 tripwire regression alarm run across modules; each module exposes its output for ablation.

**Data-profiling findings that shaped Goal 4 (all 100k, re-confirmed):**
- **Positives are vanishingly rare and Stage-A recall loss is fatal:** narrative signal ‚Äî ranking/search/rec ~959 (1.0%), embeddings/vector 155 (0.2%), eval/NDCG 74 (0.07%); target titles ~0.8%. The pool is ~95% obviously-irrelevant roles (Business Analyst, HR Manager, Mechanical Engineer‚Ä¶, each ~5‚Äì6k). ‚Üí recall-first retrieval; tiny positive class + **zero labels** ‚Üí learned LTR is data-starved.
- **No labels, no leaderboard, 3 submissions** ‚Üí we are our own ground truth; tuning is audit-driven, not metric-driven.
- **Half the score is NDCG@10** ‚Üí margins at the very top matter (favors magnitude-preserving fusion over pure rank/RRF).
- **Assessment coverage only 24%** ‚Üí assessments are high-trust *when present* but can never be *required* (would silently disqualify assessment-less true positives) ‚Üí demonstrated career-evidence must be an independent satisfying source.
- **Cheap channels are nearly free** (cosine over 100k√ó384 ‚âà150 MB, BM25 mat-vec, vectorized features all sub-budget); the only "expensive" op is the cross-encoder, which is **precomputable offline** since the JD is fixed ‚Üí the cascade exists to bound expensive precompute + mirror the JD's v2, not to save runtime.

**D1 ‚Äî Boundary & output contract (LOCKED): decomposed fit bundle, decisions deferred to Goal 6.** Goal 4 emits relevance + evidence + the `engagement_modifier`/`risk_modifier` inputs; Goal 5 supplies hard flags; Goal 6 applies gates, the multipliers, and the honeypot-constrained top-100. Keeps the locked Goal 5/6/7 scopes intact and gives the eval harness clean, independently-ablatable knobs. *Rejected:* single finished score (A ‚Äî tangles gating/selection into Goal 4, less defensible/ablatable); Goal-4-applies-gates (C ‚Äî splits gating across goals, invites drift).

**D2 ‚Äî Re-ranker model fork (LOCKED): transparent weighted scorer = primary & final relevance arbiter.** *Rejected as primary:* LightGBM/LambdaMART LTR ‚Äî needs relevance labels we don't have; programmatic labels make it merely mimic our heuristic (circular, overfits noise), hand-labels give too few positives; black-box, harder to defend, latches onto honeypots. **Parked** as a future ablation only if D9 ever yields credible graded labels. **Cross-encoder reclassified** from alternative re-ranker ‚Üí optional **precomputed relevance *feature*** (bounded positive contribution; may never override structured evidence, corroboration failures, disqualifiers, or trap logic). The scorer's weakness ‚Äî **interaction blindness** ‚Äî is acknowledged and recovered *explicitly* via D6/D7 (must-have soft-ANDs, corroboration requirements, critical-criterion combinations), never an opaque model.

**D3 ‚Äî Retrieval cascade / shortlist (LOCKED): generous recall-first union.** Shortlist ‚âà **5‚Äì8k unique** via `dense_evidence_topK ‚à™ dense_identity_topK ‚à™ BM25_topK ‚à™ structural_prefilter` (K‚âà2‚Äì3k/channel). **Membership = union, not fused ranking** (RRF/fused cutoff could drop a candidate strong in only one channel ‚Äî exactly the Tier-5 strong on dense-evidence but weak on BM25; the real ranking is Stage B's job). **Structural prefilter** guarantees inclusion of strong non-text evidence (assessment corroboration, substantial product-company ML tenure) even when lexical/dense retrieval is weak. **No exclusionary gates in retrieval** ‚Äî honeypots/disqualifiers may enter; detection = Goal 5, enforcement = Goal 6. Stage B (transparent scorer) + cross-encoder features run **only** on the shortlist. *Rejected:* tight ~1‚Äì2k shortlist (fatal recall risk for compute we don't need to save).

**D4 ‚Äî Feature transforms & normalization (LOCKED): expose-raw ‚Üí declarative transform spec.** Default for skewed continuous = **quantile/rank-norm** using Goal 3's frozen full-pool percentiles, saturating beyond p1/p99 (robust to planted outliers, distribution-free, no tuning). Already-bounded features (rates, flags, normalized similarities) ‚Üí identity/simple rescale. **Non-monotonic band features (YOE, applied-ML years) excluded from generic normalization** ‚Üí explicit membership functions in D6. All transforms standardized to **higher = better fit** (lower-is-better inverted post-transform). **Missing ‚Üí neutral, never negative** via the Goal 3 `_present`-flag masking contract. Transforms fit on the **full 100k pool**, never the shortlist. A version-controlled spec maps each feature ‚Üí `{transform, direction, params, neutral}`. Finer percentile grids deferred unless audits reveal a calibration problem (optimize for simplicity/stability/reproducibility first).

**D5 ‚Äî Score fusion & calibration (LOCKED): hierarchical, criterion-satisfaction spine.** The **structured criterion-satisfaction score is the fit spine**; BM25/dense/CE act **primarily as evidence sources for criterion satisfaction** (D7), not independent additive channels (flat fusion *rejected* ‚Äî a high dense cosine alone would inflate fit = the keyword-stuffer hole). On top, **dense-evidence + CE add small, capped, audit-tuned global bonuses** (Tier-5 safety net) that may refine but never dominate fit. **Relevance channels use tail-anchored calibration** against full-pool distributions (pin the irrelevant *bulk* ‚âà p90‚Äìp95 ‚Üí 0, reward only the upper tail ‚Üí 1; preserves additive-positive *and* magnitude ‚Äî quantile-norm rejected for relevance because it hands the irrelevant median 0.5). **Pool-level BM25/dense/CE distribution statistics emitted offline** for reproducible, shortlist-independent calibration.

**D6 ‚Äî Importance‚Üíweights, must-have combination & band functions (LOCKED).** Importance‚Üíweights via a **fixed tiered ladder** by class (critical/high/medium/low ‚Üí one number each; per-criterion override only where an audit demands; exact values audit-tuned). Must-haves combine via **weighted geometric-mean soft-AND** (any near-zero must-have drags the block down, but no single thin-evidence axis zeros a genuine fit; floor Œµ; *coverage-multiplier rejected* as more brittle). Nice-to-haves = **subordinate, capped, multiplicative** (`√ó(1+NH_bonus)`) so they differentiate only candidates who already clear the must-haves and can never compensate a must-have failure. Context modifiers (YOE, applied-ML years, location, notice) = **trapezoidal membership ‚Üí bounded multiplicative nudge** (`CM_modifier`, clamped). Aggregation: `structured_criteria_score = MH_softAND √ó (1 + NH_bonus) √ó CM_modifier`. Multiplicative subordination is itself an interaction guard.

**D6.5 ‚Äî Engagement modifier (LOCKED): bounded multiplier computed in Goal 4, applied in Goal 6.** Captures **availability + responsiveness only**. Core (high wt): `recruiter_response_rate`, `last_active_recency` (inverted, quantile ‚Äî recency is relative), `open_to_work_flag` (soft positive, not a gate). Secondary: `interview_completion_rate`, `avg_response_time` (inverted), `applications_submitted_30d`. Market-demand (low, cautious): `saved_by_recruiters_30d`. **Credibility signals split out** (verifications, completeness, offer_acceptance ‚Üí Goal 5 risk / separate nudge, not engagement). `engagement_modifier = clamp(1 + (E ‚àí E_median)¬∑s, 0.7, 1.0‚Üí1.1)` ‚Äî **asymmetric [0.7, 1.1]**, more downside than upside (a ghost loses ~30%; a star gains only ~10% ‚Üí engagement can never manufacture a top pick). Missing ‚Üí neutral (1.0). The **multiplicative-on-fit structure is the real defense**: low-fit √ó high-engagement stays low ‚Üí kills the sample_submission "active but irrelevant HR-Manager" trap.

**D7 ‚Äî Corroboration ‚Üí criterion satisfaction (LOCKED): trust-tiered noisy-OR with a soft gate.** **Three trust tiers** by fakeability: Tier-1 **Verified** (`skill_assessment_scores`, `assess_{c}_max ‚â• Œ∏_assess`; high-trust but only 24% coverage ‚Üí never required); Tier-2 **Demonstrated** (career-history evidence: dense-evidence cosine + career-*description* lexical, up-weighted when title/role/company consistent ‚Äî the universal hard source protecting assessment-less Tier-5s); Tier-3 **Declared** (summary/headline lexical, title tokens, skills-array ‚Äî the stuffer's toolkit). **Skillmeta = modulator only** (raises confidence of an existing hit, never creates one). **Strong satisfaction requires ‚â•1 Tier-1-or-2 source**; **Tier-3-only evidence is smoothly attenuated toward a small floor** (not a hard ceiling). Aggregation = **noisy-OR** of trust√óstrength contributions (`raw = 1 ‚àí ŒÝ(1‚àíc·µ¢)`), √ó a **soft corroboration gate** `smoothramp(n_independent/min_sources)` so thin evidence is depressed, not annihilated. **Validated on both adversarial cases:** stuffer ‚Üí Tier-3 only, no Tier-1/2, `n<min_sources` ‚Üí satisfaction ~floor ‚Üí soft-AND collapses ‚Üí low fit; plain-language Tier-5 ‚Üí Tier-2 (¬±Tier-1) satisfies must-haves with zero buzzwords (no buzzword-absence penalty anywhere, additive-positive since Goal 2 D4).

**D8 ‚Äî Divergence & anti-profile penalties (LOCKED): bounded soft-risk layer, distinct from Goal 5.** `risk_penalty` from **two independently-capped sub-penalties** (kept separate to avoid hidden double-counting): (a) **stuffer geometry** ‚Äî `relu(lexical_norm ‚àí dense_evidence_norm)` + `identity_evidence_divergence` (native to Goal 4); (b) **archetype resemblance** ‚Äî anti-profile cosines (identity & evidence) to {keyword-stuffer, research-only, cv_speech_robotics, consulting-only, recent-llm-only}, combined as **max + small blend bonus** (not pure max, not weighted average). Mapping: penalty-only, **`risk_modifier = clamp(1 ‚àí f(risk_penalty), 0.5, 1.0)`** ‚Äî soft risk degrades fit smoothly, floor 0.5 (annihilation reserved for Goal 5/6). **Monotone, no overrides** ‚Äî the JD's "unless" clauses (pre-LLM ML rescues recent-llm; product stint rescues consulting) live only in Goal 5's decisive layer; a soft false-positive is harmless because the bounded penalty is dominated by strong corroborated fit. **Non-double-count contract:** D7 = *earn positive credit*, D8 = *soft suspicion penalty*, Goal 5 = *hard decision* ‚Äî deliberately different magnitudes, reinforcement = robustness not destructive compounding; D8 owns the continuous‚Üípenalty *mapping*, Goal 5 owns the hard *threshold/flag*.

**D9 ‚Äî Offline eval / self-labeling harness (LOCKED): audit-driven, tripwire-guarded.** **Human top-k audits are the optimization target** ‚Äî standing scorecard: top-~50 hand-categorization (strong/plausible/weak/trap), must-have coverage, trap/stuffer & honeypot incidence in top-10/100, inter-iteration movement diffs. Additions (incl. the cross-encoder feature) earn inclusion only if they improve audits **without increasing trap exposure** ‚Äî **never** evaluated on estimated NDCG. A stratified **~250‚Äì400 hand-labeled tripwire set** (enriched with suspected honeypots, stuffers, narrative-concept hits, random background) has the **full competition metric computed on it but is explicitly a regression ALARM only ‚Äî not an optimization target, not usable for weight-search or model selection.** Plus a **trap/adversarial unit-test suite** (canonical stuffer ranked down, Tier-5 up, each honeypot archetype excluded ‚Äî pass/fail each iteration) and **3-submission discipline** (one change at a time; prefer robust over audit-maximizing weights; freeze before final; treat submissions as {safe baseline, tuned, final}).
