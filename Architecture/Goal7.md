### Goal 7 — Reasoning generation — ✅ FULLY DETAILED & LOCKED (D1–D8)
**SCOPE:** turn the top-100 selection into the CSV `reasoning` column — a 1–2 sentence justification per candidate. Goal 7 is a **deterministic evidence-to-narrative compiler**: it consumes an already-decided evidence contract from Goals 4–6 and *projects* it into prose. It **never reads raw candidate data and never selects evidence** — so reasoning can never drift from the ranking logic. Runs **at runtime inside the budget** (5 min / 16 GB / CPU / no network), but cost is trivial (100 rows). Judged at **Stage 4** on 10 random rows against 6 checks: specific facts, JD-connection, honest concerns, no hallucination, variation, rank-tone consistency.

**Data-profiling findings that shaped Goal 7 (all 100k):**
- **title / YOE / current_company / summary present for 100%** → every row always has a citeable identity anchor and real narrative facts (summary ~463 char median) to extract.
- **`skill_assessment_scores` only 24.2%** → Verified-tier corroboration is high-trust *when present* but never assumable; **`skills[]` diluted ~12k×/skill → NEVER citeable** (naming a skill from it = the exact "hallucination" the rubric penalizes).
- **notice_period present 100%, >60d for 61.6%** → an honest, citeable concern is essentially always available; honest-concern coverage is never the bottleneck.
- **Concept rarity** (ranking/search ~1%, embeddings ~0.2%, eval ~0.07%) → feeds the *distinctiveness* term so rare corroborated hits are foregrounded in the explanation.

**D1 — Generation method (LOCKED): deterministic evidence-to-narrative compiler, not a template engine.** Input = a structured evidence contract (IR). Intermediate representation = facts tagged with **roles** (`primary_strength`, `secondary_strength`, `jd_link`, `concern`, `context_modifier`). Surface realization = a rank-conditioned **phrase grammar**. *Rejected:* extractive draft + LLM polish (no legitimate offline phase — top-100 is only known post-ranking; hosted LLM violates no-network; hand-polish breaks single-command reproduction); in-budget local LLM (reintroduces hallucination, nondeterminism, Stage-3 fragility for cosmetic gain on 100 rows). **Invariant:** reasoning is a pure projection of already-decided evidence.

**D2 — Evidence contract + two-stage split (LOCKED).** Goal 7 = **7A Explanation Planner** (deterministic; role-tags & orders the contract into the IR) + **7B Surface Realizer** (phrase grammar → string). Contract per selected candidate: decision/identity block (`candidate_id`, `rank`, `final_score`, `fit_score`, **`fit_tier`** [emitted by Goal 4], `filler_flag` ≡ `fit_tier==Filler`; `rank_band` retained audit-only); always-on anchor literals (`current_title`, `years_of_experience`, `current_company`+`company_type`, `product_tenure_yrs`); per-criterion **strength evidence** `{criterion_id, jd_label, class, importance, satisfaction_degree, fit_contribution, evidence:[{evidence_id, source_tier (verified/demonstrated/declared), source_type, field_path, raw_value, surface_snippet}]}` (the `jd_label` makes JD-connection automatic); modifiers (`engagement_modifier`+top signal, `risk_modifier`+top component, `consulting_gate_suppressed`+`suppression_evidence`); audit-only `stuffer_suspect_flag`. **Every evidence atom carries a stable `evidence_id`** (minted offline in Goal 3, stable across the pipeline); the planner binds each rendered clause to its `evidence_id` (provenance side-artifact, not in CSV) → mechanically auditable grounding. `surface_snippet` is **precomputed offline in Goal 3** for criterion-linked narrative spans, and is null when none exists → the realizer falls back to structured facts (runtime never reopens raw text). Stuffer/honeypot diagnostics are **audit-only, never rendered**; `suppression_evidence` IS rendered as positive context. *Rejected (Option B):* Goal 6 emits the role-tagged IR — pushes presentation into frozen Goal 6 and splits the compiler across goals.

**D3 — Explanation Planner internals (LOCKED).** **Explanation-strength** (ordering of what to *mention*, distinct from scoring weight) = **`fit_contribution × trust_tier_factor × distinctiveness × citeability`**. **`fit_contribution` = the criterion's *positive* contribution to `fit_score`, defined as normalized `wᵢ·satᵢ`** (its weighted satisfaction as a share of the total) — **NOT** log-share/marginal-drag, which in a soft-AND foregrounds the *weakest* axis (the opposite of a showcase strength). Rank-drivers float to `primary_strengths` by construction → **no must-have guard needed** (rejected). The complement `wᵢ·(1−satᵢ)` identifies the weakest axis and feeds **concern-ladder rung 4** — one quantity serves both strengths and concern. `trust_tier_factor` rewards Verified/Demonstrated over Declared; `distinctiveness` uses frozen pool concept-frequency stats; `citeability` rewards a concrete snippet over vector-only. **Concern-priority ladder (deterministic, first applicable):** (1) `filler_flag` → below-cutoff acknowledgement; (2) material surviving soft-risk; (3) context-modifier miss (notice>60d / location / YOE band-edge / stale engagement); (4) **weakest-evidence deficit** (framed as an *evidence deficit in recruiter language*, e.g. "limited demonstrated evidence of production retrieval at scale" — never a criterion name, never "missing", always "lighter"); (5) none material. One primary concern; tone-calibrated by band (D5).

**D4 — Citeable-field whitelist (LOCKED).** Realizer may cite ONLY: anchor literals; `skill_assessment_scores` (named skills allowed *only* here, Verified tier); career narrative snippets (titles/descriptions, Demonstrated tier); `redrob_signals` values. **Raw `skills[]` array is never citeable.** Every cited token must resolve to an `evidence_id`.

**D5 — Rank-band tone calibration (LOCKED): four bands, upstream-driven.** Goal 7 consumes a **semantic `fit_tier` ∈ {Elite, Strong, Plausible, Filler}** emitted upstream (scorer owns the boundaries; Goal 7 computes nothing from raw scores → single source of truth). Tone tracks **fit quality, not seat position.** Concern rendering conditioned on band: **Elite** → concern optional, framed as a *consideration*; **Strong** → framed as a *caveat*; **Plausible** → concern *required*; **Filler** → *limitation + below-cutoff acknowledgement required*. *Rejected:* fixed rank cutoffs (ties tone to position → over-praise risk); raw-score thresholds inside Goal 7 (breaks single-source-of-truth).

**D6 — Surface Realizer (LOCKED): slot-grammar, evidence-driven first.** Sentence skeleton (≤~240 char): **S1** = adaptive anchor + 1–2 primary strengths with JD link; **S2** = secondary/engagement + band-conditioned concern. **Evidence-driven phrase selection first**, `candidate_id`-hash **tiebreak only** for residual phrasing variety. **Separate realization paths per trust tier** (Verified "scored 88 on the IR assessment" / Demonstrated "shipped vector search at Razorpay" / Declared hedged "describes…/lists…"). **Adaptive anchor** — not always title+YOE+company; lead with whatever is most informative (sometimes the strength leads). Variation primarily **content-driven**, banks secondary; all rendered facts stay bound to `evidence_id` provenance. **Snippets render only when precomputed (Goal 3); otherwise the realizer falls back to structured facts** (title/YOE/company/assessment/signals) — graceful degradation, never a gap. *Rejected:* single fixed frame (the penalized template); lightweight CFG (awkward-output risk, harder to defend).

**D7 — Validation & anti-hallucination harness (LOCKED): two tiers + audit report.** **Hard invariants (fail the build):** grounding/evidence resolution (every cited token resolves to an `evidence_id`; named skills ∈ assessment set; numbers equal source fields; zero `skills[]` tokens), numeric correctness, provenance completeness, uniqueness, CSV validity. **Soft diagnostics (warn only):** diversity, skeleton concentration, tone distribution, phrase-bank usage. Cheap invariants run **in-runtime as hard asserts** (non-empty, unique, length, every clause has `evidence_id`, finite — loud fail like other goals); richer checks run in the offline build/audit harness. Emits a **`reasoning_audit_report.json`** flagging suspicious rows for human review (no LLM judge — human-in-the-loop, consistent with Goal 4 D9). Satisfies Goal 6 validation #8 (reasoning-rank consistency) and #11 (selection provenance).

**D8 — Output mechanics & reproducibility (LOCKED).** Reasoning folds into Goal 6's `submission.csv`; Goal 7 never touches `score` (Goal 6's raw `final_score`). **Length: adaptive 1–2 sentences, ~240-char cap** — S1 always; S2 only on a real secondary strength or a required concern (band rules drive structure; no padding on thin profiles). *Rejected:* always-two-sentences (forces empty S2 = soft-templating); tight single sentence (too dense). Write via `csv.writer` **`QUOTE_ALL`**, UTF-8, snippet newlines collapsed to spaces. Fully **deterministic** (evidence-driven selection + pinned `candidate_id`-hash tiebreak; version-controlled phrase banks + grammar; no RNG). Side artifacts: `reasoning_provenance.json` (clause→`evidence_id`), `reasoning_audit_report.json`; manifest discipline (sha256, grammar/bank version, git commit, timestamp) mirrors Goals 1–6.
